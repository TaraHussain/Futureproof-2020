The fragrance

Égoïste is a men's perfume produced by French fashion house Chanel. It was created by perfumer Jacques Polge. The perfume was released in 1990. The word is French for "egoist", which means selfish or self-centered. Commercials for the perfume have been directed by Jean-Paul Goude.

The iconic fragrance notes

Egoiste by Chanel is a Woody Spicy fragrance for men. The nose behind this fragrance is Jacques Polge. Top notes are Brazilian Rosewood, Coriander, Sicilian Mandarin and Mahogany; middle notes are Cinnamon, Damask Rose and Carnation; base notes are Sandalwood, Tobacco, Vanilla, Leather and Amber.
